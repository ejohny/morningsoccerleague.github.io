Format & Logistics

  1. Games are 7v7 a side
  2. Games are 25 minute halves with a 5 minute half-time break
    Description Start Time End Time
    Reporting Cut Off Time 6:10
    1st Half 6:20 6:45
    Break 6:45 6:50
    2nd Half 6:50 7:15
  3. Long weekends will not have any games scheduled
  4. Franchisee Owner need to assign a captain for the team who is responsible for the team on the ground. Franchisee owner can be the Captain himself or he can designate any player from the team. The captain can be changed for each game though

Rules

  1. Reporting
    1. Team captains to report to Referee 10 minutes before kickoff time. Forfeit would be declared if at kick off any team has lesser than 5 players who have reported
    2. Home team will be given choice to pick either kick off or pick a side for the game
    3. Team Captain and one another player need to help setting up the posts from the Home team. If the Home team 2 folks are not available for setting up goal, its considered as forfeit
  2. Substitutions
    1. Unlimited substitutions of players are permitted, when the ball is out of play (throw- ins and goal kicks)
    2. A substituted player can come in again and be substituted again
  3. Equipment/Jersey
    1. Red color bibs will be provided for Home team
    2. Yellow color bibs will be provided for Away team
    3. Blue color bibs will be provided for the Referees
    4. Goal Keeper for each team should bring their own Jersey/bib which is neither Red/Yellow/Blue (Preferably white)
    5. Goal Keeper should have preferably the Keeping Gloves
    6. Soccer Cleats and Shin guards are required for all the players. No exceptions
  4. Play is governed by FIFA rules unless otherwise noted
  5. No offside rules
  6. Sliding/Slide Tackling
    1. Field players are not allowed to do any slide-tackling or sliding what so ever
    2. Unintentional sliding is also called a foul, basically will leave up to the Ref to decide the same
    3. If the player is down(somehow due to shoulder push or any other reason) and then tries to play the ball while on the ground is also considered as sliding
    4. Goalkeepers can slide-tackle ONLY if clearly going for the ball within his penalty area. If he is playing the ball outside his penalty area, he is essentially a field player and same sliding rules for field players apply to the goal keeper
  7. Goal Keepers
    1. Goal Keepers can be changed with a player on field or substituted multiple times
    2. They can be changed/substituted ONLY on the following events (throw-ins and goal kicks)
  8. Referees
    1. Each Franchisee need to send one each Referee for the game allotted for them as the Referee
    2. Two referees will referee each game. One referee will take care of one half and other will take care of the other half. They are always welcome to talk each other before taking the decision, if needed
    3. If the Referees doesn’t show up for the game
      1. the team who is supposed to send the Referee will earn one minus points for the team
      2. the teams can plan for a day to play the game before next Friday
        1. if both teams agree to play on same day, the game will happen and the Referee team should sent the Referees for this game again
        2. If both teams doesn’t agree, then they split the points
  9. Cautions/Ejections
    1. Any excessive contact or collision will result in a foul and ejection of player
    2. Any player abusing the referee will automatically be ejected from the game
    3. A total of 4 yellow cards will result in 1 game ban
    4. A red card in the game will result in immediate ejection of the player from the game and will have a 1 game ban as well
  10. Points calculations
    Description Points
    Win 3
    Draw 1
    Loss 0
    Forfeit before 8 PM(Prior day) -0.5
    Forfeit after 8 PM(Prior day) -1
    Referee - No Show -1
    1. Score of 3-0 goes to winning teams for forfeits
  11. Weather related or Getting Kicked out due to ground constraints
    1. If the game doesn’t happen due to weather related issues or due to unavailability of ground, the game will be postponed to the last weekends after all the games are completed
  12. The Franchisee Owners would be informed about any updates/changes
  13. Mid-season Draft
    1. Any players interested in joining the league now, will be put into a Registered, but available players pool
    2. There will be a mid-season draft that will be held at the mid-season and all the players registered will be picked on same basis as the Draft Day for the season. Basically, a lottery will be held and teams will pick plyers according to the lottery seed
  14. Championship
    1. The team who gets Highest points will be considered as Champions
    2. In case 2 or more teams have same points, highest goal difference will be considered
    3. In case of them being same, highest Goals scored for will be considered
    4. In case of them being same, Head on Head score (both home and away will be considered together)
    5. Despite all these, if everything is same a coin toss will decide the winner
